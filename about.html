About This Project

The Underground IT Toolkit is a static educational blog created for IT students and beginners in tech. Its goal is to bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world IT practices.

Here, you'll find resources about GitHub, debugging, cloud tools, and soft skills that universities often overlook but employers highly value.
